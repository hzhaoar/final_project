Tomori Takamatsu

Tomori Takamatsu is a first-year student at Haneoka Girls' High School and the vocalist of MyGO!!!!! and the vocalist and lyricist of MyGO!!!!!.

Youmiya Hina is the voice actress of Tomori Takamatsu and vocalist of MyGO!!!!! in reality.

Appearance

Tomori has short, chin-length ash brown hair and rose-gold eyes.

Her original band outfit consisted of an oversized white jacket with two vertical blue stripes on each sleeve, black stripes just above the cuffs, kangaroo pockets on each side, and four chartreuse yellow straps hanging from the bottom. Underneath the jacket, she wears a black turtleneck top and black shorts. She wears blue-and-white shoes with white laces over black socks.

In her casual outfit, she wears a dark blue and pale yellow hoodie, a white T-shirt, short dark blue shorts, and black-and-white sneakers.

Background

Tomori was born to mother Hikari and father Yoshiji.

Ever since she was young, Tomori had felt like she didn't belong anywhere, and had difficulty making friends due to her awkward personality. She thought that she was always a step behind everyone else.

In middle school, specifically in her 3rd year, Tomori was invited to form a band by Sakiko after a chance meeting with the latter. This led to her forming a band called CRYCHIC together with Mutsumi, Sakiko, Soyo, and Taki and she acted as their vocalist and lyricist. However, after their first and only live performance, the band broke up, leaving Tomori in a distraught and broken state, and she kept blaming herself for the band's breakup.

Personality

Tomori is a kind but sensitive girl who, as mentioned above, has an awkward personality. She has a tendency to stutter while talking and not make eye contact. She also tends to blame herself for the conflicts that happen around her, even if they weren't caused by her.

She is infamously known as "Haneoka's Weirdo Girl" due to her peculiar personality. She likes collecting all sorts of things, such as band-aids (especially ones with animals such as pandas or penguins on them), slippery things, just-the-right-size things, and even rocks and fallen leaves that she picks up from the ground. She also likes pill bugs and has been shown often drawing them in her notebooks.
