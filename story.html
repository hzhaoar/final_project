Band Story

Warning: This section contains spoilers for the anime BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!!. Click the button below to hide or show episode descriptions:

Episode 01: Haneoka's Weirdo Girl

Due to unforeseen circumstances, Anon transfers into Haneoka Girls' High School just before Golden Week. Knowing that bands are popular in Haneoka, she hurries to find some band members. In the process, she ends up running into "Haneoka's Weirdo", and attempts to recruit her...

Episode 02: I Won't Invite You Again

Anon asks Tomori to create a band with her, but the latter refuses. While searching for band members at RiNG, she disastrously meets a certain girl again. "She clicked her tongue at me!"

Episode 03: CRYCHIC

"I want to be a human." Tomori feels like she doesn't belong anywhere due to her awkward personality. During the spring of ninth grade, she ends up meeting someone under Styrax obassia flowers...

Episode 04: For Our Whole Lives?!

Anon learns about the disbandment of CRYCHIC from Soyo and decides to invite her, Tomori and Taki to talk with her at RiNG.

Episode 05: I'm Not Running Away!

The girls formed the band with the promise of not disbanding until their first live concert. However, Anon isn't taking practice seriously, leading to Taki asking her if she's just running away.

Episode 06: Why, When It's Too Late

Everyone is surprised by Raana's sudden declaration of wanting to be in the girls' band. Meanwhile, Taki's patience starts wearing thin as the band's practice for the concert isn't going as smoothly as she'd hoped.

Episode 07: Even after Today's Concert Ends

It is finally the day of the concert and things are hectic for the band. With trembling legs, Tomori finally stands onstage again. "I may be lost, but I'll keep on moving as the lost."

Episode 08: Why

Upon seeing Sakiko's tearful reaction to Tomori and the others performing "Haruhikage" unexpectedly, Soyo decides to cut contact with all of them. In the meantime, Sakiko calls Uika and meets up with her. "So you still love stars."

Episode 09: Disbanding

Soyo has always acted according to others' expectations of her. As such, CRYCHIC was everything to her... "We didn't actually disband."

Episode 10: Always Lost

The band has fallen completely apart. While at unease about the band's future, Tomori runs into Uika at a planetarium. "I believe that you can convey feelings through songs."

Episode 11: Even So

Everyone in the band was moved by Tomori's heartfelt song, and its disbandment has been avoided. However the girls don't have time to take a breather as their next concert has already been scheduled. "Hey, wait a sec! Haven't you guys forgotten something more important and urgent?"

Episode 12: It's my go!!!!!

The girls have reunited and formed their "band of lost girls". Although they managed to complete both their new song and costumes, they are still feeling quite anxious about their upcoming concert. "We finally got to perform properly like we wanted to, but is this really it?!"

Episode 13: The Only One I Can Trust Is Myself

MyGO!!!!! decides to celebrate the success of their live performance at RiNG. "May you find happiness."
