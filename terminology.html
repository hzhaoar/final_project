Terminology

BanG Dream!

BanG Dream!, also known as Bandori, is a Japanese music media franchise owned by Bushiroad. Created by Bushiroad president Takaaki Kidani in January 2015 with original story by Kō Nakamura, the project began as a manga before expanding to other media. In addition to other manga, BanG Dream! includes an anime television series, live concerts, singles and albums, VTubers, and the mobile rhythm game BanG Dream! Girls Band Party! by Craft Egg.

Girls Band Party!

Girls Band Party! is a rhythm game released for iOS and Android, based on the BanG Dream! franchise. It is published and developed by Bushiroad Games. It was released on March 16, 2017 for iOS and Android devices in Japan.

BanG Dream! Ave Mujica

BanG Dream! Ave Mujica is an anime in the BanG Dream! series, solely dedicated to the band Ave Mujica. It will begin airing on January 2, 2025. It was announced on September 14, 2023, after the airing of BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!!'s final episode.

CRYCHIC

CRYCHIC was an all-girl band in the BanG Dream! franchise. The group consisted of five members, namely Takamatsu Tomori on vocals, Wakaba Mutsumi on guitar, Nagasaki Soyo on bass, Shiina Taki on drums, and Togawa Sakiko on keyboard.

Later after the band's disbandment, Tomori, Soyo and Taki would end up forming MyGO!!!!! along with Chihaya Anon and Kaname Raana, and in BanG Dream! It's MyGO!!!!!'s 13th episode, Sakiko and Mutsumi would be revealed to be a part of Ave Mujica.
