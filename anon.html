Appearance

Anon is someone of average height and has light gray eyes and long, waist-length pink hair. She sometimes wears glasses, especially at home.

Her original band outfit is a black dress with two vertical blue stripes on each side. The sleeves are mesh, and each cuff has a chartreuse yellow ribbon tied on it. She wears this dress with a black belt, silver buckle, and black lace-up boots with blue shoelaces.

In her casual outfit, she wears a sleeveless gray plaid dress over a long-sleeved white blouse. The dress has a similarly patterned belt around the middle with a black buckle. She also wears a necklace with a heart charm on it.

Background

When Anon was in middle school, she was the student council president and was known as a popular and talented girl, however she had no close friends and everyone referred to her with her last name. Due to the aforementioned facts, her schoolmates would constantly put her on a pedestal. Near the end of ninth grade, she had expressed an interest in attending a high school in England as a foreign exchange student. However, while there, she realized how inferior she was in contrast to everyone, and she was also very bothered by the fact that everybody, including teachers, referred to her as "Ann" and not Anon. This then led to her moving back to Japan and transferring into Haneoka at an odd time.

Personality

Anon is an honor student who is very social and also assertive at the same time. Despite this, it is shown that she has had a hard time making friends. Anon also loves following and trying out the newest trends. She approaches Tomori after learning about her in hopes of creating a band together with her.

Although she initially created the band in order to gain attention, to the point where she wanted to name the band after herself, she ends up becoming dedicated to her band activities and taking practicing seriously.

Anon often tries taking the position of the band's leader, however her ideas tend to be shrugged off (mainly by Taki and Soyo) or receive little to no response or encouragement.
